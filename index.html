How does Metaquest work

METAQUEST is a blockchain-based WebGL game deployed on the ethereum blockchain. The story of Metaquest goes back to a very long time ago, where a hero who will be you, is on a quest through the metaverse completing small quests through our sidescroller game to reach the final destination. Holding MQ tokens in your wallet allows you to play the game to earn, all you have to do is connect your wallet with the tokens to the game's website and you are set to go on the Quest!

  • Adventure.
  • Arena Battle.
  • Breeding.

$MQ tokens are used to power the eco-system of the Meta-Quest game $MQ website will also be used for staking tokens to earn even more rewards, and the more tokens you hold the higher your spot will be on the whitelist for an nft drop the project will be doing,more info on that later on!

$MQ tokens can be acquired on uniswap, and as long as you have them in your wallet you can participate in the game Be sure to checkout the Roadmap on the bottom of the website !
